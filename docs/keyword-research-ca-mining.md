## 8. Competitor Gap Insights

### miningpeople.com.au and mining-recruitment.com
Both competitors returned ERROR 50 across all three SEMrush databases (ZA, UK, US). Neither is indexed in the SEMrush crawl at a level that produces keyword data.

**Implications:**
1. There is no directly trackable competitor in SEMrush for the Africa mining recruitment niche. CA Mining is operating in an underindexed market — precisely where first-mover SEO advantage is strongest.
2. miningpeople.com.au is Australia-focused; its lack of ZA indexing suggests minimal presence in the African mining search landscape despite the Australian expat workforce overlap.
3. The competition density values (`Co` field) from phrase_this calls remain the best available proxy. Most CA Mining target terms show `Co` 0.01–0.19 — very low real-world competition in ZA mining SERPs.

### ZA search landscape context
The related keyword analysis reveals the actual competitive context in ZA mining searches:
- **Direct-hire mine brands dominate:** Harmony Gold, Northam Platinum, Sibanye Stillwater, Implats, Assmang, Glencore all appear in related terms — job seekers searching for specific mine vacancies.
- **Job boards are the intermediaries:** Pnet.co.za appears in related terms across multiple clusters (`pnet co za login page`, `pnet careers`) — they are the dominant competitor for job seeker traffic in ZA.
- **CA Mining's gap:** Pnet captures broad job-seeker traffic; mine brands capture direct-apply traffic. CA Mining's opportunity is positioning as the specialist recruiter between the two — for complex roles, executive placements, expat/FIFO searches, and cross-border Africa mandates where generalist job boards fall short.
