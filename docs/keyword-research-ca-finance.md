## 6. Cluster Structure

### Pillar 1 — Finance Jobs Hub (SA & Africa)
**Primary keyword:** `finance jobs` (ZA: 1,900 vol, comp 0.10)
**Supporting keywords:** finance jobs south africa (590 vol, comp 0.09), financial manager jobs (1,300 vol, comp 0.12), finance executive jobs (20 vol, comp 0.13), finance jobs africa (20 vol, comp 0.33)
**Content angle:** Central finance job search hub for South Africa and Africa. Aggregates CA Finance's active vacancies, covers financial manager, executive finance, and broad finance role searches. CA Finance has no current ranking for any of these terms — the highest-volume opportunity in this research.
**Priority:** Highest — 1,900 vol at comp 0.10 with zero CA Finance presence. Combined pillar volume exceeds 3,800.

### Pillar 2 — C-Suite Finance Recruitment (CFO / Finance Director)
**Primary keyword:** `CFO recruitment` (US: 210 vol, CPC $12.34 — highest CPC in research)
**Supporting keywords:** cfo jobs (ZA: 480 vol), cfo jobs south africa (320 vol), cfo search (UK: 70 vol, CPC $9.99), finance director recruitment (UK: 260 vol), finance director jobs south africa (ZA: 110 vol), executive search finance (UK: 140 vol, CPC $2.75), executive search africa (ZA: 20 vol), finance executive jobs (ZA: 20 vol)
**Content angle:** C-suite service page targeting clients hiring CFOs, Finance Directors, and senior finance executives across African markets. The $12.34 CPC signals maximum commercial intent — PE-backed businesses, multinationals, and major corporates searching for executive finance search partners. CA Finance's CA(SA) credentialed leadership is the key differentiator.
**Priority:** High — CFO recruitment is the single most commercially valuable keyword in this research ($12.34 CPC). No CA Finance ranking exists.

### Pillar 3 — CA(SA) & Accounting Talent
**Primary keyword:** `chartered accountant jobs` (ZA: 720 vol, comp 0.06)
**Supporting keywords:** CA SA jobs (ZA: 480 vol, comp 0.06), accounting jobs south africa (ZA: 480 vol, comp 0.09), chartered accountant jobs south africa (260 vol, comp 0.09)
**Content angle:** CA(SA) and chartered accountant specialist recruitment page — targeting both job seekers (CA(SA) professionals seeking roles) and clients (companies seeking chartered accountant talent). CA Finance is the natural owner of this niche by brand name alone, yet ranks for none of these terms. Combined ZA volume across four terms exceeds 1,940.
**Priority:** High — comp 0.06 on the primary is very low for 720 vol. Near-zero competition quick win with the highest relevance-to-brand of any pillar.

### Pillar 4 — Development Finance & DFI Sector
**Primary keyword:** `dfi` (ZA: 590 vol, CPC $3.56, comp 0.02 — near-zero competition)
**Supporting keywords:** development finance jobs (ZA: 110 vol, comp 0.03), development finance institutions dfis (ZA: 90 vol, CPC $4.01, comp 0.06), dfi jobs (ZA: 20 vol, comp 0.33)
**Content angle:** CA Finance uniquely serves the Development Finance Institution sector in Africa — a specialism no generalist recruitment firm covers at depth. CA Finance already ranks #3 for `development finance jobs` and #23 for `dfi` (590 vol, CPC $3.56). A dedicated DFI hub page with roles content consolidates these rankings and captures high-CPC search intent from development banks, MFIs, and DFI operators.
**Priority:** High — best existing foothold in the dataset ($3.56–$4.01 CPC, near-zero comp, partial rankings to build from). Best ROI per word of content.

### Pillar 5 — Investment Banking & Private Equity
**Primary keyword:** `private equity jobs` (ZA: 210 vol, comp 0.06)

CA Finance already ranks #11 for `private equity jobs south africa` and #16 for `private equity jobs` — both weak positions on an existing page. Dedicated service content will improve both rankings and capture the investment banking recruitment international audience.
**Priority:** Medium-High — existing partial rankings to build on; low competition across all terms.

### Pillar 6 — Specialist Finance Roles (Risk, Treasury, Control)
