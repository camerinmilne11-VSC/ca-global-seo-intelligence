## 5. Competitor Gap Insights

### michaelpage.co.za
SEMrush returned ERROR 50 (no US data) — domain not indexed in the US database. SA-focused domains are not tracked in the US database. This is expected for local .co.za sites.

### kornferry.com (primary competitor signal)
Korn Ferry is the dominant global executive search player in the US database. They rank #1 for:
- `executive recruiting search firms` (6,600 vol, CPC $4.76)
- `executive headhunting firms` (5,400 vol)
- `executive search firms` (5,400 vol)
- `executive recruiters` (4,400 vol)
- `executive recruiting firms` (2,400 vol)
- `legal recruiter` (8,100 vol, CPC $5.82)
- `recruitment process outsourcing` (3,600 vol, CPC $17.15)

**Gap strategy:** CA Global cannot out-compete Korn Ferry on brand volume, but CAN compete on:
1. Africa geo-targeting (Korn Ferry has no Africa-specific content depth)
2. Niche verticals: mining, African finance, African C-suite
3. "Lists and comparisons" content that positions CA Global alongside Korn Ferry
4. Low-competition variants: `executive search agency` (KD 15, CPC $5.14), `executive search consultants` (KD 15, CPC $3.60)

These are too niche for the US database — they may have data in the `za` or `uk` database.
- Competitor analysis limited to US-indexed domains; `.co.za` competitors (Michael Page, Robert Walters SA) are not tracked in the US database.
- Volume figures are monthly averages; treat low-volume Africa-specific terms as directional signals, not precise targets.
