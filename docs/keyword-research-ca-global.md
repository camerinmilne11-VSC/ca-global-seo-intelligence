## 7. Competitor Gap Insights

### .co.za Competitors — ZA Database
SEMrush returned ERROR 50 for all tested SA domains in the ZA database:
- `michaelpage.co.za` — not indexed
- `humancapital.co.za` — not indexed
- `robertwalters.co.za` — not indexed

**Strategic implication:** The SEMrush ZA database does not have sufficient crawl data on SA-specific executive recruitment domains. This means the ZA market is **less competitively mapped** than global markets — CA Global can potentially rank for ZA terms without strong tracked competitors. The competition density values (Co) from phrase_this calls are the best available proxy: most ZA terms show Co 0.03–0.43, indicating low to moderate real-world competition.

### kornferry.com (US database — global benchmark)
Korn Ferry remains the dominant global signal. Ranks #1 for:
- `executive recruiting search firms` (6,600 vol, CPC $4.76)
- `executive headhunting firms` (5,400 vol)
- `executive search firms` (5,400 vol)
- `executive recruiters` (4,400 vol)
- `executive recruiting firms` (2,400 vol)
- `legal recruiter` (8,100 vol, CPC $5.82)
- `recruitment process outsourcing` (3,600 vol, CPC $17.15)

**Gap strategy:** CA Global cannot out-compete Korn Ferry on US volume terms, but CAN dominate:
1. ZA database SA-specific terms (Korn Ferry likely absent from local SA SERPs)
2. Africa geo-targeting (Korn Ferry has no Africa-specific content depth)
3. Niche verticals: mining jobs Africa (210 vol, comp 0.05), expat jobs Africa
4. Low-competition US variants: `executive search agency` (KD 15), `executive search consultants` (KD 15)
