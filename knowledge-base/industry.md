# Recruitment Industry Knowledge Base

## Executive Search vs Recruitment
- **Executive search (headhunting)**: proactive, candidate-led, typically C-suite and senior director level. CA Global's core offering.
- **Contingency recruitment**: reactive, vacancy-led, success-fee only. Used for mid-level roles.
- **Retained search**: client pays upfront, exclusive mandate. Higher commitment, higher service level.

## African Recruitment Markets
- **South Africa**: largest formal recruitment market in Africa. High demand for finance, mining, and engineering.
- **Sub-Saharan Africa**: fast-growing markets in Nigeria (finance/tech), Kenya (finance/FMCG), Ghana (mining/oil & gas), Tanzania, Mozambique, Zambia (mining).
- **North Africa**: Egypt, Morocco — significant but different regulatory environment.
- **Key challenges**: skills gaps, visa/work permit complexity, currency risk, infrastructure variation.

## Mining Recruitment (CA Mining)
- Commodities covered: gold, platinum, copper, coal, iron ore, lithium, cobalt, diamond, chrome.
- Key roles: Mine Manager, General Manager, Mining Engineer, Metallurgist, Geologist, Safety Manager, Plant Manager, Finance Manager.
- Key markets: South Africa, DRC, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, West Africa (Ghana, Guinea), Mozambique.
- Industry context: ESG pressures, automation/AI adoption, energy transition (battery metals boom), junior vs senior mining companies.

## Finance Recruitment (CA Finance)
- Disciplines: Financial Management, Investment Banking, Asset Management, Treasury, Audit, Tax, Corporate Finance, Risk, Compliance.
- Seniority: CFO, FD, Finance Manager, Financial Analyst, CA(SA), ACCA, CFA holders.
- Key markets: South Africa, Mauritius, Kenya, Nigeria, rest of Africa + international relocations.
- Industry context: IFRS adoption, Basel requirements, fintech disruption, ESG reporting demands.

## Candidate Attraction Strategies
- Passive candidates: most executive-level talent is not actively job seeking. Headhunting is essential.
- Value proposition: career growth, compensation benchmarking, confidentiality.
- African diaspora: significant talent pool of African professionals in UK, Europe, USA open to return.

## Employer Branding
- Clients increasingly need employer brand positioning to attract passive talent.
- EVP (Employee Value Proposition) development is a differentiator for retained mandates.

## Key Terminology
- ATS: Applicant Tracking System
- EVP: Employee Value Proposition
- KPIs: Key Performance Indicators
- NED: Non-Executive Director
- CA(SA): Chartered Accountant (South Africa) — highly sought after
- ACCA: Association of Chartered Certified Accountants
- CFA: Chartered Financial Analyst
- RPO: Recruitment Process Outsourcing
- PSL: Preferred Supplier List
