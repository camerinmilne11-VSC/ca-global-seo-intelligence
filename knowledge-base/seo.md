# SEO Playbook — CA Global, CA Mining, CA Finance

## On-Page Standards
- Title tag: primary keyword near the front, brand name at end with pipe | CA Global
- Meta description: 140–155 characters, include primary keyword, write for clicks not just crawlers
- H1: exactly one per page, matches or closely reflects title tag keyword
- H2s: include secondary keywords naturally, use question format where appropriate
- H3s: supporting details, long-tail terms

## Keyword Usage
- Primary keyword: in title, H1, first 100 words, one H2, and naturally 2–3× in body
- Secondary keywords: distributed across H2s and body naturally
- Avoid keyword stuffing — write for the reader first
- Use semantic variations and LSI terms throughout

## Internal Linking
- Every article must link to at least 2 relevant internal pages
- Link to service pages where contextually appropriate
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Pillar pages receive the most internal links

## Content Length Guidelines
- Informational articles: 1,200–1,800 words
- Comprehensive guides: 2,000–3,500 words
- News/commentary: 600–900 words
- Location/niche pages: 800–1,200 words

## E-E-A-T Implementation
- Include author bio on all articles
- Cite specific data, statistics, and sources
- Reference our recruitment experience and track record
- Use first-person plural ("we placed", "our clients") to demonstrate experience

## Recruitment SEO Specifics
- Target long-tail geographic + role combinations: "[role] recruitment [country/region]"
- Candidate-focused: job titles + "jobs in [location]" or "careers in [sector]"
- Client-focused: "[sector] recruitment agency [location]" or "hire [role] in [location]"
- FAQ sections signal E-E-A-T and capture featured snippet opportunities

## Content Refresh
- Update ranking content every 6–12 months with fresh statistics
- Add new FAQ items based on Search Console queries
- Expand thin sections when competitors rank above us
