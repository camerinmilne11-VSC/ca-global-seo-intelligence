# Humour Guidelines — CA Global Brands

## Overall Assessment
CA Global, CA Mining, and CA Finance operate in executive and senior-level recruitment. The brands are professional, credible, and relationship-driven. Humour is not a core element of the brand voice.

## Where Humour Is Acceptable
- Light, self-aware observations about industry quirks (e.g., "If you've sat through a three-round interview process for a role that was filled internally, you're not alone")
- Mild irony in conversational thought leadership pieces
- Warm, human moments in candidate-facing content only (never in client-facing service pages)

## Where Humour Must Be Avoided
- Any content about specific roles, candidates, or clients
- Technical SEO or compliance-related content
- Salary guides, market reports, or statistics
- Headlines and meta descriptions (these must be direct and keyword-rich)
- LinkedIn or social posts representing the brand formally

## Unacceptable
- Sarcasm about hiring processes, candidates, or competitors
- Jokes about African business culture or geography
- Self-deprecating humour about the recruitment industry
- Anything that could read as unprofessional to a CFO or CEO

## Safe Test
If a C-suite candidate or client read the piece, would they respect the brand more or less? If less — remove the humour.
