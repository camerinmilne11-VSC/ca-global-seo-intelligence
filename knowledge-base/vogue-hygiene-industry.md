# Vogue Hygiene — Industry Knowledge Base

## What the company does

Vogue Hygiene provides commercial cleaning, sanitation, and hygiene services to corporate, commercial, and industrial clients across South Africa. The company operates in Cape Town, the Western Cape, and Gauteng. Services are tailored to each client's environment and delivered with an emphasis on environmental responsibility and quality management.

Core service lines:
- Commercial and office cleaning
- Industrial and factory cleaning
- Hygiene and sanitation solutions (including bathroom services)
- Waste disposal and waste management
- Event cleaning (pre- and post-event)
- Deep cleaning and specialist cleaning
- Eco-friendly and biodegradable cleaning using environmentally safe products

## Who the clients are

Target clients are businesses, not consumers. The typical buyer is an operations manager, facilities manager, or procurement manager at a:
- Corporate head office or multi-floor office building
- Factory, warehouse, or manufacturing plant
- Retail or hospitality venue
- Event venue or conference centre

Decision drivers: reliability, compliance with health and safety regulations, competitive pricing, and a proven track record. Environmental credentials are increasingly important — especially for companies with ESG or sustainability mandates.

## The South African cleaning industry

The commercial cleaning sector in South Africa is large and competitive, dominated nationally by players like Bidvest Facilities Management, Servest, and Compass Group. Regional and specialist companies compete on price, responsiveness, and flexibility.

Key dynamics:
- The Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) No. 85 of 1993 governs workplace hygiene and cleanliness standards. Non-compliance exposes businesses to fines and reputational risk.
- HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points) standards apply to food-handling environments — relevant for factory and kitchen cleaning clients.
- The shift toward green procurement means more corporates now require ISO 14001-compliant or eco-certified cleaning providers.
- Post-COVID, standards for sanitisation and hygiene auditing have permanently risen. Clients expect documented cleaning schedules, checklists, and compliance certificates.

## Geographic context

Cape Town and Johannesburg/Gauteng are the two primary markets. Each has distinct dynamics:
- **Cape Town / Western Cape:** Strong corporate and events sector, tourism-driven hospitality, significant industrial activity in the Cape Winelands and Bellville corridor
- **Gauteng:** Largest economic hub in Africa; high density of corporate offices, factories, warehouses, and logistics centres in Sandton, Midrand, and the East Rand

## Tone notes for this brand

Vogue Hygiene is a professional B2B service company. Content should be:
- Practical and solution-focused — readers are time-poor operations managers
- Confident without being aggressive — peer-to-peer, not sales-heavy
- Technically credible — cite actual regulations, industry standards, realistic cost ranges where relevant
- South African in framing — reference rand costs, local regulations, local geography

Avoid recruitment language. Avoid generic "we are passionate about cleaning" filler. Every piece of content should help the reader make a decision or understand something they didn't before.
